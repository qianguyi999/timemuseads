Our Story

Preserving Heritage Through Commerce

We believe that the world's most precious cultural treasures deserve to be celebrated, shared, and sustained for generations to come

The Art of Heritage Commerce

Founded by artisans and business professionals, TimeMuse was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to create a sustainable ecosystem for intangible cultural heritage. We witnessed too many beautiful crafts fading away due to lack of market access and modern business expertise.

Today, we work with over 70 master artisans across 12 provinces in China, providing practical business solutions that allow them to focus on what they do best—creating extraordinary works of art that carry centuries of cultural wisdom.

From cloisonné enamel to filigree silverwork, from jade carving to lacquerware, we are committed to bringing the finest traditional crafts to discerning collectors and customers nationwide.
